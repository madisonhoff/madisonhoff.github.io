About Me!

I am a student at Lehigh University. I'm studying journalism and computer science with the goal to find a job that can combine the two fields. In one of my journalism classes, we worked with data, data tools online, and Excel sheets to create visualizations of the data. I learned that don't just have data just because you want data, but find a question first and then use data and the right tool to write a story on this subject.

Interactive visualization

Putting data on are chart may not be too helpful to the viewer. They may want to look at a specific data point or to focus on one variable. Interactivity within the data visualization is therefore important. I looked at how factors like ethnicity and race have played a role in the number of Lehigh graduates. T create this visualization, I used Tableau Public , which allows users to create interactive visualizations for free with an account.
