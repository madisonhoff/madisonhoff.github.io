About Me!

I am currently a student at Lehigh University. I'm studying journalism and computer science with the goal to find a job that can combine the two fields. In one of my journalism classes, we worked with data, online data tools, and Excel or Google sheets to create visualizations of the data. I learned that you should not just include data in a story just so you have some statistics, but find a question first and then use data, if necessary, and the right tool to write a story on this subject.

Interactive visualization

Putting data on a chart may not be too helpful to a viewer. They may want to look at a specific data point or to focus on one variable. Interactivity within the data visualization is therefore important. I created an interactive line chart about Lehigh graduation rates by ethnicity and race. To create this visualization, I used Tableau Public , which allows users to create interactive visualizations for free with an account.
