University Communication

Putting data on a chart may not be too helpful to a viewer. They may want to look at a specific data point or to focus on one variable. Interactivity within the data visualization is therefore important. I created an interactive line chart about Lehigh graduation rates by ethnicity and race. To create this visualization, I used Tableau Public , which allows users to create interactive visualizations for free with an account.
